About Us

Who We Are

Vecron Exim is a leading global trade finance company, providing short term, pre-shipment and purchase order finance. Our loans typically carry 30, 60 or 90 day terms, and support industries such as agrifood, textile, consumer goods and many more.

Vecron Exim was founded through collaboration between former trade industry executives, highly skilled financiers and successful Silicon Valley VCs. Having worked within and alongside these industries, we have accumulated deep understanding of the complications trade businesses and start-ups face when interacting with conventional banks.

Our Leadership

As an independent finance company with vast international trade and business experience, we are able to make prompt decisions that are aligned with our partner’s objectives, throughout the supply chain. Our diversified management integrates different industry experiences and expertise, and provides a unique perspective from which we can customize an ideal solution.

Vecron Exim is devoted to generating a full spectrum of innovative solutions for diversified trade finance challenges. It is our mission to be an industry leader in the development, execution, implementation and support of tailor-made financial structures that suit the entire range of the supply chain. Vecron provides more than just money, we also provide the added value of our expertise in logistics and compliance, to both export and import companies.
